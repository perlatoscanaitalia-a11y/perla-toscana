# Perla Toscana - Project Overview

## Website Objectives

The website must become the official digital home for Perla Toscana and the primary channel for direct bookings.

Primary objectives:

- Present Perla Toscana as an elegant holiday apartment near Florence, Chianti, The Mall Firenze and Figline e Incisa Valdarno.
- Convert qualified visitors into direct booking requests.
- Reduce dependency on OTAs over time.
- Build topical authority for local accommodation searches around Florence, Chianti, The Mall Firenze and Valdarno.
- Give travelers enough practical information to choose Perla Toscana confidently.
- Communicate that Florence can also be reached without relying entirely on a car.
- Become the most useful local accommodation website for guests before and during their stay.

## Business Goals

- Increase direct bookings through the official website.
- Capture high-intent travelers searching for accommodation near Florence, Chianti, The Mall Firenze and Figline Valdarno.
- Support family and group stays up to 8 guests.
- Communicate real amenities clearly, including private parking, fast Wi-Fi, air conditioning, kitchen, Smart TVs and included linens/towels.
- Build trust through real photos, reviews, CIN/CIR, policies and direct contact.

## Target Audience

Primary users:

- Families visiting Tuscany by car.
- Groups up to 8 guests.
- Couples or small groups planning Florence and Chianti itineraries.
- Travelers visiting The Mall Firenze Luxury Outlets.
- International guests looking for a self-catering apartment near Florence.
- Guests who prefer a quieter base outside the Florence historic center.
- International guests arriving by train or plane who want to visit Florence without handling city parking.

## Direct Booking Strategy

The site must guide users toward direct booking at every key decision point.

Primary direct booking actions:

- Main `Prenota` / `Book Direct` button.
- WhatsApp contact button.
- Availability/request section.
- Booking CTA after services, gallery, location, reviews and FAQs.

Direct booking messaging must be transparent. Do not claim "best price" or "no commissions" unless confirmed by the owner and legally safe.

## Brand Positioning

Perla Toscana is positioned as:

- Elegant but not formal.
- Premium but not ostentatious.
- Relaxing and authentic.
- A practical Tuscany base for Florence, Chianti and The Mall Firenze.
- A comfortable apartment for families and groups.
- A direct-booking alternative to anonymous OTA listings.

The website must feel like a trusted local host, not a generic travel portal.

## Guest Information Hub

The website must include a Guest Information Hub designed to reduce pre-arrival questions and improve trust.

The hub should cover:

- Public transport.
- Taxi and private transfers.
- Airports.
- Railway connections.
- Supermarkets nearby.
- Pharmacies.
- Hospitals and medical assistance.
- Emergency numbers.
- EV charging stations.
- Petrol stations.
- Restaurants nearby.
- Wine tours and private drivers.
- Arrival guide.
- Food & Drinks Nearby.

Rules:

- Keep the information practical and evergreen.
- Do not publish fixed prices, opening hours, bus timetables or provider availability unless there is a reliable live source.
- Encourage guests to contact taxi, transfer, restaurant, wine-tour and service providers directly for current prices and availability.
- Link back to Homepage, Location, FAQ, Book Direct, Arrival Guide, Family, Florence and Chianti pages.

## Food & Drinks Nearby

The Guest Information Hub must include a Food & Drinks Nearby section that helps guests quickly find places to eat, drink and buy food.

Required sections:

- Breakfast Cafes.
- Bakeries.
- Pastry Shops.
- Traditional Tuscan Restaurants.
- Family Restaurants.
- Pizzerias.
- American Diner.
- Wine Bars.
- Ice Cream Shops.
- Supermarkets.

Confirmed recommendation:

- 1950 American Diner - FIGLINE V.NO.
- Suitable for lunch and dinner.
- Wide parking.
- 1950s atmosphere.
- Very appreciated by families and children.

Do not publish fixed opening hours, prices or phone numbers. Every recommended place should use an `Open in Google Maps` button so visitors can see live hours, reviews, photos, navigation and phone number.

## Public Transport Advantage

Perla Toscana should communicate public transport as a practical advantage, especially for Florence visits.

Verified information:

- Bus stop: SR69 Deposito Gas
- Stop IDs: FIV074 and FIV075
- Distance from Perla Toscana: approximately 200 metres walking
- Route logic: bus to Figline Valdarno railway station, then train to Florence Santa Maria Novella

Do not publish fixed bus timetables because they may change. Link visitors to the official Autolinee Toscane website for current schedules: <https://www.at-bus.it/>
