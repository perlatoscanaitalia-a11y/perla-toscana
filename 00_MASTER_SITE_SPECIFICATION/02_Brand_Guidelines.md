# Perla Toscana - Brand Guidelines

## Visual Identity

The visual identity must feel elegant, relaxing, premium, minimal and authentically Tuscan. It should suggest quality and calm without looking expensive or overdesigned.

## Color Palette

Primary:

- Olive Green: `#556B4D`

Secondary:

- Warm Ivory: `#F8F5EF`

CTA:

- Warm Gold: `#C89B3C`

Accent:

- Terracotta: `#B56A4A`

Text:

- Dark Gray: `#2F2F2F`

Usage rules:

- Use Warm Ivory as the dominant page background.
- Use Dark Gray for body text.
- Use Olive Green for navigation, headings accents, section dividers and calm brand moments.
- Use Warm Gold only for primary CTAs and important conversion elements.
- Use Terracotta sparingly for small accents, tags, icons or hover states.
- Avoid heavy gradients, purple/blue palettes, beige-only layouts and excessive decorative effects.

## Typography

Headings:

- `Cormorant Garamond`

Body:

- `Inter`

Rules:

- H1 should be elegant and spacious, but not oversized on mobile.
- Body text should be highly readable.
- Do not use negative letter spacing.
- Avoid viewport-based font scaling.
- Keep line length comfortable, ideally 60-78 characters for long text.

## Spacing

Use a consistent spacing scale:

- `4px` micro spacing
- `8px` compact spacing
- `16px` component spacing
- `24px` section internal spacing
- `40px` small section spacing
- `64px` standard section spacing
- `96px` major section spacing on desktop

Mobile pages should keep sections compact and scannable.

## Border Radius

- Buttons: `6px`
- Cards: `8px` maximum
- Images: `6px` or `8px`
- Inputs: `6px`

Avoid overly rounded pill styling unless used for small labels or language selectors.

## Buttons

Primary CTA:

- Background: Warm Gold `#C89B3C`
- Text: Dark Gray or white, depending on contrast
- Label examples: `Prenota`, `Book Direct`, `Verifica disponibilita`

Secondary CTA:

- Border: Olive Green
- Text: Olive Green
- Background: transparent or Warm Ivory

WhatsApp CTA:

- Use a recognizable WhatsApp icon if available.
- Keep the style consistent with the brand; avoid overly bright green unless required for recognition.

Rules:

- Primary booking action must be visually stronger than secondary actions.
- Buttons must have hover, focus and disabled states.
- Buttons must never shift layout on hover.

## Cards

Cards are allowed only for repeated items, reviews, attraction previews, service previews and booking/FAQ modules.

Rules:

- Do not place cards inside cards.
- Do not turn every section into a floating card.
- Use subtle borders or low-opacity shadows.
- Keep card radius at `8px` or less.

## Icons

Use simple line icons, preferably from the same icon library throughout the site.

Icon usage:

- Services grid
- Buttons where appropriate
- Feature highlights
- Contact methods
- FAQ affordances

Rules:

- Icons must support meaning, not decorate randomly.
- Keep stroke weight consistent.
- Avoid custom mixed-style icons.

## Shadows

Use shadows sparingly.

Recommended:

- Soft, low-opacity shadows for image cards or overlays.
- Prefer borders and spacing over heavy shadows.

Avoid:

- Large floating shadows.
- Glossy effects.
- Dark overlays that hide important property details.

## Image Style

Images must feel real, bright and inspectable.

Rules:

- Use real property photos wherever possible.
- Avoid dark, blurred, overly cropped or stock-like imagery.
- Show actual rooms, beds, kitchen, bathroom, parking and surroundings.
- Hero images should immediately communicate the property or Tuscany setting.
- Do not promote the garden until it is ready.

## Overall Design Principles

- Fast and minimal.
- Elegant without clutter.
- Conversion-focused without feeling aggressive.
- Mobile-first.
- Clear navigation to Book Direct, WhatsApp, Location, Services and Gallery.
- Every section should answer a traveler question or reduce booking hesitation.
