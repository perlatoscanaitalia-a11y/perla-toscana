# Perla Toscana - Content Standards

## Page Structure

Every page must have:

- Clear H1.
- Short introduction.
- Main content sections.
- Relevant images.
- Internal links.
- Booking CTA.
- FAQ section when useful.
- Trust signal near conversion areas.

Money pages should include:

- Hero.
- Key benefits.
- Location relevance.
- Amenities.
- Gallery preview.
- Reviews or trust block.
- FAQ.
- Book Direct CTA.

Authority articles should include:

- Practical travel advice.
- Clear itinerary or answer.
- Links to relevant landing pages.
- No generic filler.

## Tone of Voice

Tone must be:

- Warm.
- Clear.
- Elegant.
- Practical.
- Calm.
- Locally knowledgeable.

Avoid:

- Overhyped luxury language.
- Generic tourism copy.
- Aggressive sales language.
- Unsupported claims.

## Paragraph Length

- Keep paragraphs short.
- 2-4 lines on desktop is ideal.
- Use lists for practical details.
- Use tables only when they improve clarity.

## CTA Placement

Every important page should include CTAs:

- Above the fold.
- After key benefits.
- After gallery or services.
- After FAQs.

CTA examples:

- Prenota direttamente
- Verifica disponibilita
- Scrivici su WhatsApp
- Book Direct

## FAQ Format

FAQ sections must answer real traveler questions.

Rules:

- Use concise answers.
- Do not duplicate the exact same FAQ on every page unless needed.
- Make FAQs visible on the page if using FAQPage schema.
- Questions should match the page's intent.

Required FAQ topics across the site:

- Distance from Florence.
- Distance from The Mall Firenze.
- Parking.
- Wi-Fi.
- Air conditioning.
- Number of guests.
- Bedrooms.
- Check-in.
- Check-out.
- Direct booking.
- Family suitability.
- No pets.
- No smoking.
- No parties.
- Public transport to Florence.

## Public Transport Content Standard

Any public transport section must be practical and evergreen.

Include:

- SR69 Deposito Gas bus stop.
- Stop IDs FIV074 and FIV075.
- Approximately 200 m walking distance from Perla Toscana.
- Bus connection to Figline Valdarno railway station.
- Train connection to Florence Santa Maria Novella.
- Benefits of visiting Florence without worrying about parking.
- Benefits for international guests arriving by train or plane.
- Link to Autolinee Toscane for current schedules.

Do not include fixed timetables or departure times.

## Guest Information Hub Content Standard

Guest Information pages must be practical, concise and easy to use during a stay.

Each page should include:

- What the guest needs to know.
- How it relates to Perla Toscana.
- When to contact a provider directly.
- Relevant internal links.
- A small CTA back to Book Direct, Location or Arrival Guide.

Required pages:

- Public Transport.
- Taxi & Private Transfers.
- Airports.
- Railway Connections.
- Supermarkets Nearby.
- Pharmacies.
- Hospitals and Medical Assistance.
- Emergency Numbers.
- EV Charging Stations.
- Petrol Stations.
- Restaurants Nearby.
- Wine Tours and Private Drivers.
- Arrival Guide.
- Food & Drinks Nearby.

Dynamic information rule:

- Do not publish fixed taxi prices, private transfer prices, wine-tour prices, restaurant opening hours, pharmacy opening hours, bus timetables or EV charger availability unless the information can be maintained.
- Prefer official links and direct provider contact guidance.

## Food & Drinks Nearby Content Standard

Every recommended place must include:

- Short description.
- Approximate distance from Perla Toscana.
- Typical type of food.
- Why guests might like it.
- Button label: `Open in Google Maps`.

Do not include:

- Fixed opening hours.
- Prices.
- Phone numbers.

Use Google Maps links so guests can see:

- Live opening hours.
- Current reviews.
- Photos.
- Navigation.
- Phone number.

Required sections:

- Breakfast Cafes.
- Bakeries.
- Pastry Shops.
- Traditional Tuscan Restaurants.
- Family Restaurants.
- Pizzerias.
- American Diner.
- Wine Bars.
- Ice Cream Shops.
- Supermarkets.

Confirmed American Diner listing:

- Name: 1950 American Diner - FIGLINE V.NO.
- Positioning: recommended for lunch and dinner.
- Strengths: wide parking, 1950s atmosphere, appreciated by families and children.
- Distance: confirm with Google Maps from the exact Perla Toscana address before publishing.

## Image Placement

Use images to prove claims.

Recommended placement:

- Hero image near the top.
- Gallery preview after key introduction.
- Room/service images near amenity sections.
- Location images only if real and relevant.

Do not use images that imply unavailable amenities.

## Review Placement

Reviews should appear:

- Homepage.
- Book Direct page.
- Reviews page.
- Near final CTAs.

Rules:

- Use real reviews only.
- Attribute reviews accurately.
- Do not fabricate ratings.
- Do not use review schema unless compliant.

## Booking Widgets

Booking or availability sections must be:

- Simple.
- Fast.
- Mobile-friendly.
- Clear about next step.

Minimum fields for a request form:

- Check-in date.
- Check-out date.
- Guests.
- Name.
- Email.
- Phone or WhatsApp.
- Message.

## Trust Signals

Use trust signals near CTAs:

- CIN/CIR.
- Real reviews.
- Private parking.
- Direct WhatsApp contact.
- Included linens/towels.
- Clear house rules.
- No pets.
- No smoking.
- No parties.
- Secure/direct booking explanation.
